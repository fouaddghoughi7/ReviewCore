# Code Of Conduct

ReviewCore is built for professional collaboration. Contributors, maintainers,
and users are expected to keep discussions respectful, focused, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different experience levels and viewpoints.
- Give feedback on the work, not the person.
- Ask clarifying questions before assuming intent.
- Keep technical discussions actionable.
- Respect project scope and maintainer decisions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors to bypass project standards.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests;
request behavior changes; or restrict participation when conduct harms the
project or its contributors.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers. Do not publish sensitive
personal information in public issues.
